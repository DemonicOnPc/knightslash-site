The Vision

KnightSlash Studios is a solo independent developer house dedicated to reviving the depth of classic RTS games with modern Roguelike intensity.

Our flagship title, Vessels of Ichor, is built on a custom NavCore architecture designed to handle thousands of units simultaneously. We believe in high-stakes gameplay where every second of the Solar Ward counts before the High Tide begins.
