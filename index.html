Core Gameplay

The Solar Ward

Helios grants a two-minute window of divine safety. Direct survivors to harvest vital resources and fortify the World-Anchor’s defenses before the light shatters.

The High Tide

As the sea inhales, Acheron’s servants rise. Lead massive-scale RTS battalions in a top-down struggle against thousands of procedural entities across crumbling peaks.

Divine Wrath

Wield devastating Godpowers to tip the scales. Channel the Powers of Helios to unleash Meteor Storms and celestial strikes to incinerate entire legions of the Rising Tide.

The Eternal Forge

Death is merely a recall to the Sun-Citadel. Offer freed Souls to the Radiant Three to permanently evolve your Ichorous-form with enhanced traits for the next Island.

Vessel Ranking

Your deeds are etched in Helios. Compete for dominance on the global leaderboards by freeing the highest tally of Souls before the island is consumed.

Defeat a God

Break the cycle. Slay Acheron’s lieutenants to open the path to the Underworld’s heart. Only by defeating the God of the Tide can you truly save the world from the abyss.

A Broken Paradise

Elysium has fallen. The ancestral continent is gone, swallowed by a relentless, primordial tide. All that remains of a golden age are the highest peaks—shattered islands rising like broken teeth above a violent, endless ocean.

"The World-Anchors are the only things keeping these final stones from slipping into the abyss. If the Anchor shatters, the island drowns. And Acheron is patient."

KnightSlash Studios

KnightSlash Studios™ is an independent development house led by a principal developer with over a decade of cross-platform technical experience. Our foundation is built upon ten years of iterative design and systems architecture, with deep expertise spanning Level Design, Project Management, and low-level systems programming in C, Java, and Rust.

The studio’s technical pedigree is rooted in extensive contributions to the indie and open-source landscapes. This includes professional asset artistry for the Voxel-RPG-MMO Veloren, alongside the development of complex systems-modifications for high-fidelity titles including Arma 3, DayZ, Stonehearth, Minecraft, and Stellaris.

Driven by the gold-standard mechanics of the Age of Empires series and the high-performance competitive frameworks of Warcraft and Starcraft, KnightSlash Studios was established to bridge the gap between traditional RTS depth and modern computational scalability.

Our flagship title, Vessels of Ichorous™, serves as the debut of our proprietary NavCore architecture. This custom-built simulation stack utilizes DOTS-first principles to facilitate real-time management of thousands of agents, delivering a performance-tier previously reserved for AAA-scale strategy titles.
