Our Studio

KnightSlash Studios™ is a solo independent developer house dedicated to reviving the depth of classic RTS games with modern Roguelike intensity.

Our flagship title, Vessels of Ichor™, is built on custom architecture designed to handle thousands of units in real-time simulation.
